New York Heritage

New York Heritage is a platform that hosts and provides access to a wide range of digital collections and cultural heritage materials related to the state of New York.These collections include photographs, manuscripts, historical documents, maps, and more, contributed by cultural institutions throughout New York State.

RICHARDSON-BATES House

Through displays and educational events, the historical Richard-Bates House provides a peek into the lifestyle of a significant and wealthy Oswego family, as well as the people and places in Oswego County. This location is an old Italian villa from the 19th century that would be a beautiful spot to visit.
